Who Am I

Obford is a solo-run consultancy. I’ve been working in IT since 18, solving problems across hardware, software, networking, and data. At 23, I’ve already delivered for industries where downtime isn’t an option.

Some of my work includes:

  • Playground Games - studio IT and custom internal software
  • Baker Hughes - industrial servers for rigs and zero-failure systems
  • Tesco - data analysis to inform new store locations
  • Terra / Wortmann - hardware support and training on server/cloud platforms

My promise: clear advice, practical solutions, and systems that just keep working.
